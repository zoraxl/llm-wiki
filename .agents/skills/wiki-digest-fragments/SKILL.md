# Wiki Digest Fragments

Use this skill to process `inbox/` without forcing every rough idea into final docs.

## Workflow

1. Read `CONCEPT.md`, `wiki/index.md`, and the relevant `inbox/` files.
2. Group related fragments by theme.
3. For each fragment, choose one action:
   - leave in inbox
   - move or group in `wiki/fragments.md`
   - convert to `wiki/open-questions.md`
   - record in `wiki/claims.md`
   - promote into an existing wiki page
   - suggest an ADR
4. Preserve original wording when it carries important product, domain, or engineering intuition.
5. Update cross-links.
6. Append `wiki/log.md`.

## Rules

- Fragments are not failures of organization.
- Do not over-polish early ideas.
- Keep contradictions visible.
- Prefer an explicit open question over a confident but unsupported synthesis.
- Do not delete raw inbox material unless it is clearly superseded and the log records where it went.

## Output

Summarize promoted fragments, deferred fragments, open questions created, claims created, and pages updated.
